# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Allison (Allie) Krier is a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2027, seeking data analyst roles in the retail industry. While she has a solid resume and LinkedIn profile, they do not effectively differentiate her from other BAIS students or fully emphasize her experiences.

The problem this product solves is the lack of a compelling, personalized way to showcase her skills, projects, and professional strengths. A personal landing page will allow her to better highlight her capabilities, present her work in more depth, and stand out to recruiters beyond what a traditional resume can offer.

## 02 — Target Audience

The primary audience for this landing page includes recruiters and hiring managers in the retail industry who are hiring for data analyst roles at mid-sized companies in the Midwest, specifically in cities such as Milwaukee, Chicago, and Des Moines.

These visitors are looking to quickly verify that Allison:
- Can analyze data using tools like SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI  
- Can apply data analysis to real business problems, particularly in retail contexts  
- Has professional experience and strong attention to detail  

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|------------------|
| Drive traffic to landing page | 25–50 unique visitors within 30–60 days |
| Increase professional connections | ~10 new LinkedIn connections |
| Generate recruiter interest | ~5 direct messages (LinkedIn or email) |
| Support job applications | ~15 applications submitted using the landing page |
